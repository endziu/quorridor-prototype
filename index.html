QUORIDOR

Objective

Be the first player to move your pawn to the opposite side of the board.

On your turn, do one of:

  • Move your pawn — one step in any direction (up, down, left, right).
  • Place a wall — blocks two cell-gaps to slow your opponent. Walls cannot completely cut off either player's path to their goal.

Controls
